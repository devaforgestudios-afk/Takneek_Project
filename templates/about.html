About Kalakar Sahayak

We are a passionate team dedicated to bridging the gap between traditional artistry and the digital world. Our mission is to empower artisans, preserve cultural heritage, and bring timeless crafts to a global audience.

Our Mission

To empower artisans and preserve cultural heritage for future generations.

Kalakar Sahayak was born from a deep-seated passion for India's rich artistic heritage and a desire to empower the talented artisans who are its custodians. We saw a world where traditional art forms were at risk of fading into obscurity, and skilled artisans were struggling to connect with a market that would appreciate and fairly compensate their craft.

Our mission is to bridge this gap. We are leveraging the power of technology not to replace but to amplify the reach and potential of traditional craftsmanship. We provide a platform where artisans can find design inspiration, price their work effectively, and connect with a global audience of art lovers.

Our Journey

The Spark (2022)

The idea for Kalakar Sahayak was born out of a trip to a rural artisan village, witnessing the incredible talent and the challenges faced by the community.

Platform Launch (2023)

After months of research and development, we launched our platform with a small group of artisans, focusing on providing essential digital tools.

Community Growth (2024)

Our community grew to over 1,000 artisans, with our marketplace gaining traction and our AI tools becoming more sophisticated.

Future Vision (2025 & Beyond)

We aim to expand our reach to every corner of India, introducing new technologies like VR galleries and expanding our global footprint.
